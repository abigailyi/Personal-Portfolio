Graphic Design

Simply Cha

This is a fictional tea brand that I created as a mini personal project.

I wanted to create a tea brand that came across as authentic, transparent about ingredients, and honest about where it's sourced and focused on sustainability. The logo should reflect honesty, simplicity, and natural good tea. This project is still a work in progress and I plan to do more work on improving Simply Cha in the near future.


Tools + Languages

Design: Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Packaging Templates from Envato Elements



View Project

Challenges

I struggled with making the product details readable with such busy backgrounds. It may look nice as a design but I thought in ways that it was also distracting and felt conflicted with how to proceed.


Solutions

My solution is to keep the labels as clean and neat as possible without trying to crowd too much information. Leave only space for the details/info that matter and try to remain true to the brand. I'm currently working on a style guide for Simply Cha to help push the project into a better direction.


Learning Points

Let the brand lead the way to making design choices.
I think I struggled so much in establishing the brand because I didn't have a clear vision and guide to give me a direction when starting this project.
